More than 15 pilots from Ecuador confirm their assistance to Santisimo DH 2015

Since the first editions, we always have seen Ecuatorian pilots in the Santisimo podiums. The 2013 title went to Ecuador with Mario Jarrin`s win.

For 2015 the race will count with UCI points, which makes the race even more atractive to South Americans. During Febuary, Santisimo has more than 15 riders from Ecuador confirming their assistance.

This Ecuatorian pilots are:

  • Alejandro Ortiz
  • Mario Jarrin
  • Arturo Montufar
  • Jonas Cordovez
  • Darwin Miranda
  • Jorge Alban
  • Marlon Quispe
  • Andrew Jerez
  • Mateo Cruz
  • Mario Cornejo
  • Belen Vargas
  • Maria Isabel Altamirano
  • Jose Carlos Espin
  • Cesar Aguirre
  • Javier Toro
  • Israel Peñafiel
  • Cristian Peñafiel

"This place is a bike paradise, there are so many long trails near to were the race takes place that coming once was not enough for me." - says Cesar Aguirre for Pro Bike Ecuador.
