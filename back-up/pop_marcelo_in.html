Marcelo Gutierrez wins Santisimo DH 2015

The multiple continental champion from Colombia took the chance to get to know Peru and race the 7th edition of the Southamerican Classic Santisimo DH 2015.

The 7th edition marked history being for the first time part of the UCI calendar for 2015. The availability of UCI points for the top riders made more people come in search of this magical points, having 50 international riders gathering a total of 9 countries represented by their pilots.

Santisimo champions from past years were present in the podium, but Marcelo Gutierrez (Redbull/Giant) did the best time putting him on the top step of the podium, no wonder why he finished 5th in the UCI World Cup in 2015.
