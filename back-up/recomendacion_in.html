  • GETTING HERE

    • The race takes place in Urubamba city, capital of the Sacred Valley of the Incas. Only one hour away from the Cusco city National Airport. The scenic Inca ruins and magical Andes will make your transfer a great trip starter. Flights to Cusco depart from Lima (Peru`s Capital) every 45 minutes starting at 5:30 am up to 17:00 pm. The airlines with most amount of daily flights to Cusco are Lan and Taca, please check:
      Lan Airline : www.lan.com Taca Airline : www.avianca.com

      Public transportation is great.  Vans and buses depart every 5 minutes from Pavitos Street in Cusco to Urubamba for US $3.00. Bikes and bike bags fit nicely on the roof racks for and extra US $ 1.00. Drivers are friendly to bikers and bikes. NOTE: vans are faster than busses and go non-stop, while buses take longer and stop several times during the way.
