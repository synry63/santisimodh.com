8TH Edition of the Santisimo Downhill Int Race will be held on paril 23 & 24th

Due to invasions happening in the finish area of our past race-trail, the race will be held in another mountain of the same district (Huayllabamba in Urubamba, Cusco).

Jobs on th e new trail will start in march but the location will remain secret until the same week the race is held, giving equal chances for riders from all over. This new race course will end in a main plaza and has great views of the Sacred Valley of the Inkas.

The official training days will be Thursday april 21 and Friday april 22, Saturday april 23 will be timed trails and the main event will be held on Sunday april 24.

Registrations will be open on line mid-March and there will be registrations on the finish line april 20-22.
