Registration

For years Santisimo DH has had online registrations on the SDH website. This year the only online registration will be via e mail. There will be a registration tent at the strat area april 21,22 and 23. There WILL BE NO REGISTRATIONS on april 24 and 25.

Kike`s Brasa Chicken restaurant located at Av Mariscal Castilla in Urubamba, will be a registration point until april 23

Facts:

  • - It is mandatory to use front and back numbers during official days april24 & 25
    - Numbers will be handed to riders April 24 on the start area starting at 9:00 am
    - It is mandatory to complete 2 runs of the track on april 24 in order to start on april 25
    - on April 24 only pilots with numbers can practice, starting at the start gat when the commissaire indicates.
    - april 23 is the last registration day.
    - it is NOT mandatory to have a 2015 UCI license to race
