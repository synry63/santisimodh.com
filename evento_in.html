EVENT

The event was born in 2009 in Cusco, Peru a place also known for being the center of the Inca Empire and the web of Inca Trails spread years ago. This our way to share with the MTB community the undiscovered Inca MTB Mecca and a chance to visit Machupichu.

April 23 & 24 will be the date for the 8th edition of the annual classic and season opener: Santisimo Downhill International DH race. In no other race will you see competitors of this caliber gathered in one awesome and historic place.

Can a Peruvian rider win for the first time the Santisimo Downhill 2016 and make the title stay at home? Find it out yourself being part of the Inca mountain biking history. Experience Santisimo Downhill.
